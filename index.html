Magnus Carlsen's 'JeansGate'

During the 2024 World Rapid and Blitz Championships, Carlsen was initially disqualified for wearing jeans, violating the event's dress code. This incident, dubbed 'JeansGate', led to widespread discussions and eventual rule changes. In a surprising turn, Carlsen auctioned the jeans for charity, raising $36,100. The proceeds were donated to Big Brothers Big Sisters of America.
